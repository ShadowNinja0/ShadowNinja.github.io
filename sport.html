Kobe Bryant wins an oscar!


Kobe Bryant, one of the most well known nba players of all time, has won made a short animation that as won him an oscar. The short film known as "Dear Basketball" was directed filmed an narrarated by Kobe himself. It is based on a letter Kobe wrote to The Player's Tribune regarding his retirement from the NBA. The plot is as follows:

"On the eve of his retirement from basketball, NBA legend Kobe Bryant describes his love for the game, which began when he was a young child. From his youthful dreams of glory to his 20-year career, Bryant describes how he and basketball have given each other all they have."

It is a truly fantastic short with great moments and a great end to a legends career.
